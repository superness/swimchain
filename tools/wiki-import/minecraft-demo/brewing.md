# Brewing

![Pixel-art potion over a brewing stand]({{media:brewing.png}})

**Brewing** is the system in [[Minecraft]] for making **potions** — drinkable or throwable items that grant temporary effects like healing, strength, fire resistance, or night vision. Potions turn tough fights and long expeditions from gambles into manageable tasks, and mastering brewing is a mark of a well-prepared player.

## The brewing stand

Potions are made at a **brewing stand**, crafted with a Blaze rod obtained from [[The Nether]]. The stand is powered by blaze powder and brews up to three bottles at once. The process starts with **water bottles**, which are turned into an **Awkward Potion** using nether wart — the base for nearly every useful potion.

## Ingredients and effects

From an Awkward Potion, a single ingredient defines the effect:

- **Glistering melon** → Healing
- **Golden carrot** → Night Vision
- **Magma cream** → Fire Resistance
- **Sugar** → Swiftness
- **Blaze powder** → Strength

**Modifiers** then tune the result. Redstone (see [[Redstone]]) extends a potion's duration, glowstone strengthens its effect, and gunpowder turns it into a **splash potion** that can be thrown to affect others — or fermented spider eye can invert an effect, turning Healing into Harming.

## Using potions

Players drink potions before a dangerous dive for [[Diamond]], before entering [[The Nether]] (where Fire Resistance is nearly essential), or before facing the Ender Dragon in [[The End]]. Splash potions of Harming double as weapons, and a splash potion of Weakness is a key step in curing a zombie villager to unlock cheaper trades from a [[Villager]].

See also: [[The Nether]] for nether wart and blaze rods, and [[Crafting]] for the bottles and stand.
