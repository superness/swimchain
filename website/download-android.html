Install Swimchain on Android

The Swimchain app runs a full node in your pocket — no server, no account. Because it's distributed as a sideloaded APK (not through the Play Store), installing takes a few extra taps. Here's the whole thing.

This is a pre-release alpha. Sideloaded apps don't auto-update — note the version and check back here (or the release notes) for new builds.

  1. Check your Android version

    You need Android 8.0 (Oreo) or newer. Settings → About phone → Android version.

  2. Download the APK

    Tap the Download the APK button above. Your browser saves it to your Downloads folder.

  3. Allow installs from your browser

    Open the downloaded file. Android will say "For your security, your phone currently isn't allowed to install unknown apps from this source." Tap Settings, turn on Allow from this source, then press back.

  4. Get past the Play Protect check

    Google Play Protect will say "App scan recommended — Play Protect hasn't seen this app before." You can tap Scan app to send it to Google first (it installs once the scan comes back clean), or tap More details → Install without scanning. This appears for any app not distributed through the Play Store.

  5. Open the app & allow notifications

    Launch Swimchain and allow the notification permission. The node runs as a background service with a persistent notification while it syncs the chain — that's how a full node stays alive on mobile.

These warnings appear for any app installed outside the Play Store — they're not specific to Swimchain. You can verify the download against the SHA-256 checksum above.
